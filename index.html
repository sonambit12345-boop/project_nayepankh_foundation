About NayePankh Foundation

We believe every individual deserves a chance to soar. Our mission is to provide wings of opportunity to those held back by circumstances.

Empowering Communities Since 2020

NayePankh Foundation began with a simple belief: that compassion, when organized, can transform lives. What started as a small group of passionate individuals has grown into a movement touching thousands of lives across India.

We focus on sustainable change through education, healthcare access, women empowerment, and environmental conservation. Every initiative is designed with community participation at its core.
